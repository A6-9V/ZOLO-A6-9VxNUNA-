# Privacy Badger Integration & GenX Trading Information

## Privacy Badger
Privacy Badger (privacybadger.org) is a browser extension that automatically learns to block hidden trackers. It is developed by the Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF), a nonprofit organization dedicated to defending digital privacy, free speech, and innovation.

### Key Features
- **Automatic Tracker Blocking**: Learns to identify and block trackers based on their behavior.
- **Privacy Protection**: Prevents third-party scripts from tracking your browsing habits across different sites.
- **EFF Supported**: Backed by one of the leading digital rights organizations.

### Recent Achievements
- **Local Blocking**: Privacy Badger successfully blocked potential trackers on `127.0.0.1`.
- **Blocked Domains**: `fonts.gstatic.com`
